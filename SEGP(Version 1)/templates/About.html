Welcome to the Turtle Streams Website!


  This is a streaming platform for the Turtle Hatchery in Chagar Hutang, Terengganu. It makes for easier detection of the hatching rate and hatching mechanisms of turtle eggs. This is through the sensors placed on top of each batch of egg clutches.


  This site implements a colour detection software on the stream, to detect change in colour of the light sensors to assist researchers in determining the hatching rate of these turtles.

Instructions to navigate around Streams tab
  1. Draw bounding box around area that requires colour detection.
  2. To confirm box, press 'space'.
  3. To move on to next box, click any key.
  4. If you have finished drawing bounding boxes on the screen, press 'q' and start detecting!



Instructions to navigate around Updates tab
  1. Type in the your query in the search box.
  2. In the drop down list, choose the group that fits your query.
  3. Click on Search.
